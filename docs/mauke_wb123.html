dataset: Mauke island lymphatic filariasis Wb123 antibody measurements

Usage

data(mauke_wb123)

Format

A data frame with 922 observations and 7 variables.

id75
individual ID in 1975

id92
individual ID in 1992

year
Year of the measurement (1975 or 1992)

age
age in years. Note that age records years completed.

CAg
Level of circulating filarial antigen (>32 is considered positive for circulating antigen)

wb123
response to the Wuchereria bancrofti Wb123 antigen

wb123pos
Seropositive, based on Wb123 using a cutoff of 10968, identified in Kubofcik et al. (2012)

Description
